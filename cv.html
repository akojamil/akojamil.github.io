Short Bio
I am a 4th year PhD student in physics with interest in fundamental interactions of particles, particularly the interactions of Neutrinos and their role in the Standard Model and beyond. My research is focused on neutrino-less double beta decay and data analysis, simulation and instrumentation related to it.

J. Dalmasson, G. Gratta, A. Jamil et al., Distributed imaging for liquid scintillation detectors, 2018
Distributed imaging for liquid scintillation detectors
We discuss a novel paradigm in the optical readout of scintillation radiation detectors. In one common configuration, such detectors are homogeneous and the scintillation light is collected and recorded by external photodetectors. It is usually assumed that imaging in such a photon-starved and large-emittance regime is not possible.
